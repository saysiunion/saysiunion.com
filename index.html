We are The SAY Sí Union, working together for a brighter SAY Sí.

Header!

On September 19, 2022, the vast majority of SAY Sí employees asked the SAY Sí board leadership to recognize their right to unionize. On October 3rd, the board formally responded, stating that while they respect the staff’s right to form a union, they will not recognize all members originally included in the proposed unit, including studio directors Stevan, Gisha, Ashley, Amalia and Anahí from admin.

This led the staff into a NLRB (National Labor Relations Board) pre-election hearing, where both the staff and the board (who chose to get legal representation by Darlene Haas-Awada of Ogletree Deakins) presented evidence in their assessments of who deserves to be in the union. As of right now, the staff patiently awaits for the final decision to be made for their case, which will likely not occur until January 2023.
